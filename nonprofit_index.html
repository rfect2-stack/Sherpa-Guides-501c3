Our Story & Vision

Sherpa Guides was founded on the belief that every individual, regardless of their starting point or perceived limitations, possesses the strength to conquer their own "summits." We are a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization driven by compassion and a commitment to action.

Inspired by the enduring spirit of the Sherpa, our navigators dedicate themselves to providing personalized support, accessible resources, and a community where every step forward is celebrated. We envision a world where barriers are diminished, and potential is limitless.
